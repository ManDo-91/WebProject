Our Story

Crafting memorable fragrances since 2010

The Beginning

Founded in 2010, Scentify began as a small boutique in Cairo with a simple mission: to bring the world's finest fragrances to discerning customers. Our founder, Sarah Ahmed, combined her passion for perfumery with years of experience in the luxury retail industry to create a unique shopping experience.

What started as a curated collection of niche fragrances has grown into Egypt's premier destination for luxury perfumes, with a carefully selected range of both classic and contemporary scents from around the world.
