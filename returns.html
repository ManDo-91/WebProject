Returns & Exchanges

Our hassle-free return and exchange policy

Return Policy Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

How to Return

  1. 1

    Initiate Your Return

    Log into your account and go to your order history. Select the item you wish to return and follow the prompts to generate a return label.

  2. 2

    Package Your Item

    Place the item in its original packaging with all tags attached. Include the return form in your package.

  3. 3

    Ship Your Return

    Attach the provided return label to your package and drop it off at any authorized shipping location.

  4. 4

    Receive Your Refund

    Once we receive and process your return, we'll issue a refund to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unused, unworn, and in their original packaging with all tags attached
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" cannot be returned or exchanged

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item for a different size or fragrance, please follow the same return process and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures you get your replacement as quickly as possible.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team immediately. We'll provide a prepaid return label and process your refund or replacement as soon as possible.

Please Note: For hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns on opened fragrances unless they are damaged or defective.
